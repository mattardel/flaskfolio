A quick Wordpress site designed to illustrate a group project on integrating health portal data with simple explanations of the meaning of their measurements. Listed next is the Flask app created for the project.

Sense Health Portal Flask App


The full Flask application built to connect patients with their health records. Utilizing Jinja2 templates for the structure, Python for the logic, and Bootstrap for styling, this web app parses a JSON database file and connects it to a user’s personal health record. Each of the listed health factors is explained and compared to the normal value ranges.


Link: Contact | github.com/mattardel/sensehealth
